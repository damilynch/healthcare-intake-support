My Standards

The backbone behind every engagement

Non-negotiable clinical registration standards — not suggestions. What separates precision revenue cycle administration from generic data entry.

🪪 6-Identifier Verification Standard

Every registration captures and verifies all six — no exceptions.

Full legal name — exactly as on Government ID, no nicknames
Date of birth — MM/DD/YYYY with 4-digit year
Government-issued ID number (not SSN as primary)
Insurance policy number AND group number
Current residential address (no PO boxes)
Primary phone number with area code

📋 7-Step Registration Workflow

Same steps, same order, every patient, every visit.

Collect documents before touching the computer
Visual verification — photo match, document validity
Data entry with source document visible at all times
Read-back all numerical fields against document
Complete insurance data character by character
Inline quality check before saving the record
Verbal confirmation of address and phone with patient

🔒 5-Step Insurance Verification

Real-time portal verification — not visual card inspection.

Collect current card — ask "Is this for today's visit?"
Access payer portal, PM system, or clearinghouse
Enter patient data exactly as shown on card
Review: status, dates, copay, network, authorizations
Document confirmation number + communicate copay

📐 CMS 1500 Mandatory Field Standard

Every mandatory field complete before services are rendered.

Core: full legal name, DOB, sex at birth, SSN
Contact: residential address, 10-digit phone
Insurance: carrier, member ID, group number, dates
Dependents: policyholder name, DOB, relationship
Benchmark: error rate below 5% · Clean claims 95%+
